Malaria Elimination Mapping

Volunteers from all over the world are contributing to eliminate malaria by mapping buildings in 500,000 square kilometers of malaria impacted communities around the globe. With properly mapped buildings, towns can allocate the right amount of mosquito prevention spray and stop malaria in its tracks.

1.7 million buildings have been mapped by 3000 contributors to help eliminate Malaria since mid-2016.
